NARRATIVE

On March 3, 2026, at approximately 2:14 PM, Officer Ramirez responded to a reported traffic
collision at the intersection of N. First St. and E. Brokaw Rd., San Jose.

Upon arrival, Officer Ramirez observed two vehicles: a 2019 Ford F-150 (Party 1) and a 2021
Honda Civic (Party 2). Party 2 vehicle sustained significant rear-end damage to the bumper
and trunk area. Airbags did NOT deploy in either vehicle.

Party 1 (Holman) stated he did not see the vehicle ahead slow down and was unable to stop
in time. Party 1 admitted he may have been distracted checking his phone navigation system.

Party 2 (Chen) stated she was stopped at a red light when she felt a sudden impact from behind.
She reported immediate onset of neck and upper back pain. She remained at the scene and
cooperated fully with officers.

No witnesses reported at the scene. One bystander, James T. Wu (contact: 408-555-0192),
stated he saw the F-150 traveling at speed before impact but did not see the collision itself.
